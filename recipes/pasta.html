Pasta Bolognese

Pasta Bolognese is a pasta dish with tomato sauce and minced meat

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Your favorite pasta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Oregano
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables in small pieces and cook them a little bit
  2. Put minced meat in the pan along with the vegetables
  3. After the meat is cooked, put tomato sauce and cook in low heat
  4. Cook for 3 hours
  5. Cook the pasta al dente and finish cooking it with the sauce
  6. Plate it and sprinkle parmesan cheese and oregano
