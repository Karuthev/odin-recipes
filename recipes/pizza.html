Margherita pizza

The pizza showed in the image is made the italian way. With a high hydration dough and cooked in a very hot oven

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Let the dough ferment and rise
  3. Flatten the dough and stretch out into a round
  4. Spread tomato sauce
  5. Sprinkle Mozzarella cheese
  6. Bake the pizza in the oven
  7. Add olive oil and basil after cooked
