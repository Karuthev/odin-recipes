Rice Three Delights

Chinese fried rice with omelet, ham, peas and carrot

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Ham
  • Peas
  • Carrot
  • Two eggs
  • Shrimps
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in boiling water
  2. While the rice is being cooked prepare an omelet with two eggs
  3. Cook the vegetables and the shrimps together
  4. After everything is cooked mix it all together
  5. Pour some soy sauce and serve
